Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Rajasthan Government Calendar 2026, holidays, and holiday policies.

How many total holidays does Rajasthan have in 2026?

Rajasthan has 50 total holidays in 2026:

  • 31 Gazetted Holidays - Official public holidays when government offices are closed
  • 19 Restricted Holidays - Optional holidays for employees to observe based on community/religious preferences

What's the difference between gazetted and restricted holidays?

Gazetted Holidays (Public Holidays):

  • Mandatory days when all government offices are closed
  • All employees automatically get paid leave
  • Banks, post offices, and most businesses are closed
  • Examples: Republic Day, Independence Day, Holi, Diwali

Restricted Holidays (Optional Holidays):

  • Employees choose based on religion or community
  • Usually one holiday per year, subject to approval
  • Government offices typically remain open
  • Examples: Lohri, Buddha Purnima, Ganesh Chaturthi

Are private offices closed on gazetted holidays?

While government offices and banks are legally required to close on gazetted holidays, private companies have more flexibility. Most private sector organizations follow government holidays, but some may:

  • Operate with a skeleton staff
  • Have different holiday policies
  • Provide alternative paid leave instead

Recommendation: Check with your HR department for specific holiday policies at your organization.

When are government offices closed in Rajasthan?

Government offices in Rajasthan are closed on:

  • All 31 Gazetted Holidays - Regular offices will display "closed" notices
  • Weekends - Saturday and Sunday (typically)
  • Second Saturday - May be closed depending on government policy

For exact office timings and closures, contact your specific government office or department.

How do I choose a restricted holiday?

For Government Employees:

  1. Review the list of 19 restricted holidays available in Rajasthan for 2026
  2. Choose a holiday that aligns with your religion, culture, or community
  3. Inform your HR/administrative department well in advance (usually 30 days)
  4. Submit the required form or request letter
  5. Once approved, use it for your observance

For Private Sector Employees: Check your organization's policy for restricted holiday procedures. They may have different rules.

Are banks closed on gazetted holidays?

Yes, all banks (public and private) are mandatorily closed on all 31 gazetted holidays in Rajasthan.

Planning Tip: If you need to conduct banking transactions, plan them on working days. Most banks offer online banking services that work even on holidays.
