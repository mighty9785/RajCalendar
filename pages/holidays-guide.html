Rajasthan Government Holidays Guide 2026

Comprehensive guide to understanding gazetted holidays, restricted holidays, and government holiday policies in Rajasthan.

Types of Holidays in Rajasthan Government

Gazetted Holidays (Public Holidays)

Gazetted holidays, also known as public holidays or national holidays, are official days declared by the Government of Rajasthan when all government offices, banks, post offices, and most commercial establishments remain closed.

Key Characteristics:

  • Mandatory closure of all government offices
  • Banks and post offices are closed
  • Employees receive paid leave automatically
  • Schools and colleges typically remain closed
  • Includes national holidays, religious festivals, and state-specific observances
  • Total in 2026: 31 gazetted holidays

These holidays are officially notified in the government gazette and are binding on all government departments and institutions.

Restricted Holidays (Optional Holidays)

Restricted holidays, also called optional or religious holidays, are days set aside for employees to observe their community, religious, or cultural celebrations. Employees can avail these holidays based on their needs and organizational policies.

Key Characteristics:

  • Employees choose based on their religion or culture
  • Typically one holiday may be chosen by each employee
  • Subject to organizational approval and policies
  • Government offices usually remain open
  • Includes religious festivals from various communities
  • Total in 2026: 19 restricted holidays

Employees must inform their organization in advance to avail restricted holidays. Private employers may have different policies.

Who Gets Rajasthan Government Holidays?

Government Employees

Gazetted Holidays: Automatically entitled to all 31 gazetted holidays with paid leave.

Restricted Holidays: Can choose one or more holidays based on personal/religious needs with approval.

Private Sector Employees

Gazetted Holidays: Most private companies follow government holidays, though they have flexibility.

Restricted Holidays: Policies vary by organization. Check with HR department.

Students & Schools

Schools, colleges, and universities typically follow gazetted holidays. Additional holidays may be declared during summer/winter breaks. Check with your institution for exact dates.

Banks & Financial Institutions

Banks are mandatorily closed on all gazetted holidays. Some restricted holidays may also result in closures depending on location and community.
