About Rajasthan Calendar 2026

Your trusted source for official Rajasthan government holidays, dates, and comprehensive holiday information.

Our Mission

Rajasthan Calendar 2026 is dedicated to providing accurate, accessible, and comprehensive information about official government holidays in Rajasthan. Our mission is to help government employees, students, businesses, and the general public plan their year effectively by offering:

  • Complete and accurate holiday dates from official government sources
  • Clear explanations of gazetted and restricted holidays
  • Interactive calendar for easy month-by-month browsing
  • Free downloadable PDF for offline reference
  • Bilingual support in Hindi and English
  • Comprehensive guides and FAQs for holiday-related queries

Information Source

All holiday dates and information provided on this website are sourced from:

  • Official Government of Rajasthan Notifications - Government circulars and gazette announcements
  • Government Office Notifications - Announcements from government departments
  • Public Holiday Declarations - Official holiday calendars published by the state government
  • Cultural and Religious Authority Sources - Verified holiday information from official sources

Disclaimer: While we strive to provide accurate information, all dates are based on official government notifications current at the time of publication. We recommend verifying critical holiday information with your respective department or organization.
